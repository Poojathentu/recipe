Chicken Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 mins

Servings: 4

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Yogurt, salt, oil
  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown, and set aside.
  3. Cook the chicken until tender.
  4. Boil rice with spices until 70% cooked.
  5. Layer rice and chicken, add fried onions, and steam for 15 minutes.
